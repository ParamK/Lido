Active Investment Management

We actively manage our clients’ investment. There’s no “set it and forget it” here. We also follow an A.C.T. approach to crafting client investment portfolios:

A - Alternatives

Sourcing investments that are not directly correlated to any one asset class.

C-Core

Qualitative and quantitative evaluation using various combinations of exchange traded funds, mutual funds and individual stocks. Top-down and bottom-up analysis pursuing alpha as compared to a benchmark.

T - Tactical

Seeking outliers from the Index. Economic cycles, market action and fundamental drivers may reveal opportunities that warrant investment consideration.
